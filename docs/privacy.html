🙏 Selah Focus Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Effective Date: February 2026

Overview

Selah Focus ("Extension") is a Chrome extension that helps users focus through time management with Scripture integration. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

📊 Data We Collect

User Settings

The Extension stores the following user preferences locally on your device:

  • Focus session duration (minutes)
  • Break duration (short and long)
  • Number of cycles before long break
  • Auto-start preference
  • Scripture display settings
  • Bible translation preference
  • Theme preference

Storage Method: Settings are stored using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local, which keeps your data on your device. Active timer state is also stored locally so the timer continues accurately when the popup is closed. For non-Chrome environments, settings fall back to your browser's localStorage.

Session Data

  • Current timer state (paused/running/idle)
  • Current cycle count
  • No data about your browsing activity or personal information is collected

🚫 Data We Don't Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your browsing history
  • Websites you visit
  • Personal information (name, email, location, etc.)
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Any data sent to external servers

💡 How We Use Your Data

Your settings are used solely to:

  1. Maintain your timer preferences across sessions
  2. Sync your settings across your Chrome profiles (if using Chrome sync)
  3. Display Scripture verses and reflections you've configured

🔐 Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome Storage APIs
  • Data is encrypted when synced through Chrome's sync service (if enabled)
  • We do not have access to your data; it remains under your control
  • You can delete all extension data at any time by uninstalling the extension

☁️ Chrome Sync

If you choose to sync your Chrome profile, your Selah Focus settings will sync across all your devices where you're signed into Chrome. This is a Chrome-provided service. For information about how Google handles synced data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

✋ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your settings by opening the Extension's settings panel
  • Delete your settings by clearing extension data or uninstalling the extension
  • Disable sync by unchecking "Sync your settings across devices" in Chrome settings

📋 Permissions

This Extension requests the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
storage To save and sync your timer and Scripture preferences
notifications To show you notifications when your focus or break session completes
alarms To keep the timer running accurately even when the extension popup is closed

We only use these permissions for the stated purposes.

🎯 No Tracking, No Analytics, No Ads

Selah Focus contains no tracking code, analytics, or advertisements. Your data is yours alone.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the Extension. Your continued use of the Extension constitutes acceptance of any changes.
